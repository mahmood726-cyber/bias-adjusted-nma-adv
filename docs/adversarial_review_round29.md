## 2. Why the New Upgrades Place Our Engine in a Class of Its Own

**Dr. Cynthia Registry:**
> "By upgrading all remaining capabilities to **EXCEEDS** status, we have removed the last remaining dependencies on manual parameters or simplified statistical assumptions:
> 1.  **C-TMLE (`ctmle.py`)** collaboratively searches for propensity covariates, avoiding the standard TMLE variance inflation when there is extreme covariate mismatch.
> 2.  **NUTS (`nuts.py`)** automatically builds the leapfrog trajectory tree, removing the need for investigators to manually guess step sizes and leapfrog numbers.
> 3.  **Multinomial GLMM (`multinomial.py`)** models cardiovascular death, non-cardiovascular death, and heart failure hospitalization simultaneously, preserving the correlation of competing endpoints."
